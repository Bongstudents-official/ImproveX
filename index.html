The 90-Minute Rule

Work in focused 90-minute blocks followed by 10 minutes of complete mental reset to keep your cognitive performance high.

Digital Eye Care

Follow the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at an object 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds to prevent screen fatigue.

Sleep Hygiene

Disconnect from screens 60 minutes before bed. Blue light suppresses melatonin, directly disrupting deep recovery cycles.

Morning Sunlight

Get 10–15 minutes of direct morning sunlight within an hour of waking to calibrate your circadian rhythm and boost daytime alertness.

Post-Meal Strolls

Take a brief 10-minute walk after lunch and dinner to significantly blunt blood sugar spikes and enhance digestion efficiency.

Brain Dumping

Write down every open task and lingering thought before starting work to free up valuable working memory and reduce anxiety.

Caffeine Cutoff

Avoid caffeine consumption at least 8 hours before sleep to prevent it from interfering with your deep restorative sleep architecture.

Daily Micro-Movement
